"I don't know where I am either ……”

“I have been in this dark castle ever since I woke up. Strangely, every day I crouched in front of the window, the only things that I could see were the dark clouds covering the earth. It’s was always rainy and thundery outside.”

"This is destined to be a dark period. “

The old bell rang again, and with the slow opening of the shabby wooden door, a black-stained diary came into view.

The above is the record in the diary, and, of course, there are more and more mysteries to be solved in this dark diary.

One thing to be realized, that, we are in a bad place. The Anglo-French century-old war ended years ago, and Joan of Arc was sentenced to fire on the cross as a heretical witch. Before the merciless cross, in the noisy crowd, only one person's heart secretly shed tears. He had indeed something to do with the whole story, but by now it's not the time to uncover that. For a few years after Joan of Arc had been put to death by fire, the ghosts wandered around the place she was executed. This diary was hidden in a villa in a high mountain which was not quiet far away from Joan’s death point. The whole building had a sense of medieval aristocracy, but the old walls were covered with moss and vines that concealed the luxuries sense. The house, which appeared to be the last place for Joan of Arc to stay in before she was sentenced, attracted a lot of "sightseeing ". As the owner of the diary said," Every night you can hear the screams, and you can see the dolls moving at will.

However, the story of the castle is far not that simple. Continuing to flip the pages, a horror story is slowly presented. How many of blood curses were there in this villa?

"On February 16, I pushed the door slowly as I was sleepy, but the locked door was now easily opened. The outside of the room is a world that I’ve never imagined, bright and luxurious. There's a long table that seems to be prepared for a lot of people to have dinner with. There are elegant candles on the table. But strangely, the flame of the candle is pure blue. “

"I continued to explore the world, through the wide living room, into the corridors. I felt like I'm dancing, and I didn't know why I'm so excited, perhaps I was just naturally interested in the outside world after being confined in a small room for long.”

"I went on walking, and in one corridor I saw a big, no, I should say a huge figure. ‘It’ slowly approached me, a little, a little, straight up to me. “

"I wanted to look up at him, but he held out his long hand and pressed me on the head. That wasn't hard, but even gave me a warm feeling. “

"He told me I was his fiancée. He himself was tired from long hours of hard work, and he didn't want his lover to see his tired face. He begged me to not look at him directly. He then asked the housekeeper who walked out of his back to show me the whole villa. Before I left, I couldn't restrain my curiosity and glanced up at his lush beard. The beard was blue, like the candles seen on the table before, fluttering with the wind, as if it were going to disappear at any moment. When I took back my conscious, the tall figure disappeared…
