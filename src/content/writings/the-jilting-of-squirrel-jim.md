Somewhere within my memory resides the squirrel Jim.
Often, we saw it jumping across the twigs
Near the window.
So vibrant, so close to us,
That it brings liveliness
To the already, lovely class.
Joe revealed its presence
In an afternoon of warm, comforting breeze
I was there, watching him,
Him looking at Jim
While Jim was staring at the class
Soon making up its mind to pass.
And time goes fast
The squirrel is deprived of its robust.
We no longer are the same us.
The squirrel far gone; his mission properly done.
Joe, on mount Evian
Yawns.
Somewhere within my sight resides the twigs dim.
Now I see them swaying in turbulent winds.
I sit
Near the window
Seeing the sky dull, so far away from me.
I am left alone.
These old days forgone.
But life carries on
People continue to climb up the mount
He was there, setting a crown
I must stair upon
Never frown.
