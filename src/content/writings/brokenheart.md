I flipped over the dusted pages of the sealed memoir. An illegible heart origami, folded by two young, foolish lovers, decorated the top. I envisioned a marriage, taking her, giving myself, until death did us part. We would be locked together in faith, with the unbreakable tie that I would cherish for life. That, we shall pledge to each other. ‘It’s been years.’ I picked up the phone, nostalgia beating through my heart. ‘Hey Alice. It's David, your fiancé!’‘I am busy!’ she almost screamed, ‘I told you yesterday, Davis, not again.’ She hung up without hesitation. Something physical cracked.
