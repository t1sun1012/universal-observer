On the throne there was the British Ruler King Authur.
With his sword Excalibur glowing, waiting for triumph of glory.
All sudden his country get suffered from a rebellion inner.
Accompanying with his divine journey ended up to be story.
It was on the hillock of Camlann, where blood scattered.
Rebellious knight Modred, came to combat using sword, shield either.
The internecine warfare caused sheen of Camlote to be wrecked.
Never known by “naïve” Modred, he was fighting his father.
Blood fog and murderous spirit enveloped the originally splendid city.
Whines could be heard everywhere, knights were screaming and shouting.
All these were caused by Modred’s loot of power and beauty.
Eventually both of their legendary life came to an ending.
Hic jacet ArthurusRex quondam Rexque futurus, his story is endless
Only in Garden Avaron, he could seek life of peacefulness…
