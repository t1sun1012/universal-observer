Perhaps the world is not made.

Sandstone upon the space, by nature, well-laid.

Perhaps nothing is made.

All the wonders of civilization, were there, will fade.

Perhaps it simply is, has, been, will always be there

The first beam of eastern glimmer glistens through the polar.

The story begins there; it comes to its end

And there again.
