I often thought that love is not a thing for me, not until I discovered my obsession with that gorgeous lark on tips of the twigs. Her feathers so delicate, and she sings as if she is a selfless vocalist. I can’t hold myself back from staring into her abstruse eyes, and her beak resembling a spear that pinpoints into my mind. Every morning I’d lean out of the window, listening all ears, just to hear how she speaks with breeze, just to find out what secret goddess she incarnates as. Appreciation a fig! We are talking about romance now.
